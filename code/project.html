Worldwide plastics production

Annual global polymer resin and fiber production (plastic production), measured in metric tonnes per year.

To understand the size of the problem of plastic pollution it's necassary to realise the rapid growth of global plastic production over the last 65 years. Since 1950, when the world produced 2 million tonnes of plastic per year, the production increased nearly 200-fold to 381 million tonnes in 2015. Prior to 1980 the percentage of plastic that was recycled was negligible, almost 100% was discarded. Since 1970 rates increased on average by about 0.7% per year. That means that in 2015 more or less 20% of the plastic waste was recycled. Besides the the 25% that was incinerated, 55% of the global plastic waste was discarded.
